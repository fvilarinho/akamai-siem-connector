
Customers want to access, analyze and process their security events to build reports, dashboards or even to get insights in real-time to take actions in a fast manner.

What if we could provide an easier and robust way to collect this data?

That's what you'll get here!

This application provides a reliable and scalable way to collect Akamai Security Events (WAF, DDoS, BOT, etc.) and easily store it into SIEMs platforms.

Follow us on https://github.com/fvilarinho/akamai-siem-connector and Feel free to contact us if you have any doubts or suggestions to improve this application. We'll be glad to help you!

So lets get started!
